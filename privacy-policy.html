Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: Streaks collects nothing. Your habits never leave your phone.

Data collection

Streaks does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your streaks and settings remain on your device and are never transmitted to any server. The app works entirely offline and does not require an account or login.

Permissions

Streaks may request permissions, such as notifications or exact alarms, only to provide core features like habit reminders. No data leaves your device.

Backups

The export feature writes a backup file to a location you choose on your own device. You control that file entirely; the app never uploads it anywhere.

Third-party services

Streaks does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.
