Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Streaks values your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your streaks and information remain on your device and are never transmitted to any server. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an account or login.

Streaks may request permissions such as notifications or storage access solely to provide core app functionality. No data is sent outside your device.
